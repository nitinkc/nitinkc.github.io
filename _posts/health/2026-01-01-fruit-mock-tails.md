**Base Liquids**: Unsweetened fruit juices (cranberry, pineapple, orange, grapefruit), coconut water, or cold brew coffee.

**Core Ingredients**: Freshly squeezed lemon and lime juice, simple syrup (or alternatives like agave or honey syrup), club soda, ginger ale, or tonic water.

**Flavor Enhancers**: Non-alcoholic spirits (e.g., Seedlip Garden, Ritual Whiskey, Lyre’s Agave Blanco), bitters (non-alcoholic or traditional), and flavored syrups such as elderflower or grenadine.

**Fresh Elements**: Mint, rosemary, basil, cucumber, or jalapeño for muddling and garnishing, adding freshness and complexity.

Optional Additions: Homemade syrups (e.g., rosemary simple syrup), marmalade cordial, or kombucha for layered flavor.

## Virgin Piña Colada
- 1 cup frozen pineapple chunks
- 1/2 pineapple juice
- 1/3 cup coconut cream

Blend with ice until smooth. Garnish with pineapple slice/wedge and cherry.

## Horchata
- rice 1 cup
- 4 cinnamon sticks
- few almonds 

Roast all of the above and soak in water overnight. 
- Blend and strain after overnight soaking. 
  - Test 1: Blend and then soak overnight
  - Test 2: Soak overnight and then blend
- Add sugar and condensed milk and milk to taste. 
- Add vanilla extract and a pinch of salt.
- Serve chilled with ice.

## Apple Cider Mocktail
equal parts ginger beer and apple cider
- Garnish with apple slices and cinnamon stick.

## Mojito Mocktail

### Prepare Mint honey syrup
- 1 cup water, 1 cup honey, handful fresh mint leaves
- Bring water and honey to a boil, stirring to dissolve honey.
- Strain and cool before using.
- keep refrigerated for 5 days.

###  Mojito with honey mint syrup
- muddled mint leaves 
- add 3 tablespoons of the mint honey syrup to 2 to 3 tablespoons of freshly squeezed lime juice 
- 3/4 cup of with sparkling water (try lime flavored sparkling water)

### Simple Mojito
- muddled mint leaves with lime juice
- top it off with lime lemon soda water or limeade

### Strawberry Mojito: 
use 3 strawberries muddled with mint leaves.

## Christmas Cranberry or Pomegranate Mocktail
- 60 ml pomegranate or cranberry juice
- 15 ml lime juice
- 120 ml ginger beer (use ginger kombucha for a twist!)
- 30 ml non-alcoholic vodka or ½ teaspoon apple cider vinegar

Pomegranate arils, lime and mint to garnish
