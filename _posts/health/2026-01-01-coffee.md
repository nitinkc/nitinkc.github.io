## Vanilla-Maple Shaken Espresso
[https://athome.starbucks.com/recipe/vanilla-maple-shaken-espresso](https://athome.starbucks.com/recipe/vanilla-maple-shaken-espresso)
- 2 shots of espresso (Blonde Espresso Roast)
- 2 pumps of vanilla syrup
- Tall glass of ice in a shaker with espresso and syrup
- Froth oatmilk and maple syrup until desired texture is reached.
  - first use the steamer to heat the milk
  - then froth it using either coffee press or milk frother.
- Top shaken espresso with maple cold foam and a pinch of pumpkin pie spice.
