## Agumbe

Agumbe is a village in the Indian state of Karnataka.
It is located in the Western Ghats mountain range and is known for its high rainfall, dense forests,
and rich biodiversity. 

Agumbe is often referred to as the "Cherrapunji of the South" due to its heavy rainfall.
